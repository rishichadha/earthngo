Raise Your Voice with Earth!

Earth NGO has been actively involved in promoting and informing about fair practices since a long time. We have an ultimate goal of corruption eradication and educating people about their rights. Creating awareness on existing policies and means to benefit out of the governmental offerings is our sub goal and we have been effective in doing so.

We bridge the gap between government agencies and the citizens in order to develop a healthy relation between them. We reach out to the youth and motivate them to participate and know more about current affairs and developmental issues as they will be the torchbearers of tomorrow.

WHAT HAVE WE DONE

  • Police Mitra Maharashtra
  • Corruption Free Maharashtra
  • Newsletter “EARTH”

It is a mobile application designed, developed for Maharashtra Police Department and managed by us, which is created for the easy functioning of the police department to maintain Law & Order in Maharashtra. Nearest police station can be GPS tracked, names of concerned Police Stations are displayed and complaints can be filed over the application with attachment of pictures.

'EARTH’ initiated a campaign “CORRUPTION FREE MAHARASHTRA” throughout Maharashtra. We used posters, SMS campaign, email campaign, social media, etc. to create awareness against corruption in the society. This campaign helped Maharashtra government to minimize corruption. Our work was recognized by United Nations and we were invited as an observer for “State Parties Convention Against Corruption” which was organized in Vienna, Austria at UNODC Head Office in the month of November in 2017.

The newsletter is an initiative to reach out to all with a realistic view of current social and political situations and spread the word of justice. It also highlights on different fundamental and legal rights which need to be known by all. People have appreciated the newsletter and have registered themselves to be our NGO ambassadors to help us help others.
